::How to play::

First, If you want to start this game, please press "Game Start" button.
And then you can play this game.
Under the canvas, there is yellow box and big jewel heart.
You have to click same heart with big heart.
If you click another heart, your score will be reduced.
And If you click bomb, your score will be zero.
So be careful to click!

The game method is same on every stage.
But the level of difficulty will increase with each stage.
I hope you have a great time with my game!
